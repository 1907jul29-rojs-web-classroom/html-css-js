To make a good coffee you need following items

Ingredients:

  • Coffee - 1 tbsp . David Offs Coffee
  • Water - 200ml
  • Sugar - 1 tbsp. use brown sugar than white as its little healthy
  • whole milk - 20 ml

Method

boil some water and add the coffee powder and brew it. After coffee is brewed add some sugar and milk. Stir it well and then pour in Mug. Enjoy every sip.
